    Who is Charles?

    Charles is a bot that asks the user questions and based on the answers, Charles recommends you the best Apple product based on your preferences.

    How does Charles function?

    Charles takes in the answers to your question and uses them to reccomend products based on those answers.

    Why is Charles needed?

    Charles is needed in order to guide people to choose the right device who may not know which products to choose from. There are many people affected by the digital devide who also do not know much about technology and Charles is there to help them choose the right device!
